Luo Jing

Luo Jing joined Hangzhou youth team system in 2006. He became one of players in the football star project and went to Portugal for football training in 2011. In July 2013, Luo returned to Hangzhou. On 16 February 2019, Luo transferred to Super League side Jiangsu FC, signing a four-year contract. “I will show my skills on the field.”

Alex Teixeira

Alex Teixeira is a Brazilian professional footballer. He began his career with Vasco da Gama, making his debut for the senior team at the age of 18, before moving to Shakhtar Donetsk in November 2009. At Shakhtar, he won the Ukrainian Premier League five times. In February 2016, he transferred to Chinese Super League side Jiangsu FC. “I want to be the legend of Jiangsu.”

Xie Pengfei

Xie scored his first goal for Hangzhou on 26 June 2012 in a 3-0 win against Shanghai East Asia in the 2012 Chinese FA Cup. On 12 February 2016, Xie transferred to Jiangsu FC. He made his debut for the club on 23 February 2016. He scored his first goal for the club on 17 September 2016 in a 2-0 win against Tianjin. “Stay positive!”
