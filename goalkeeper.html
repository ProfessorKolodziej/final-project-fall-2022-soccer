Gu Chao

Gu Chao joined the Genbao Football Academy in 2000 and was promoted to Shanghai East Asia's first team squad during the 2006 season. He played regularly as the starting goalkeeper and made an impression within the team. On 31 December 2015, Gu moved to Chinese Super League side Jiangsu Suning on a five-year contract. “I want to be the best GK in the history of Jiangsu FC.”

Zhang Yan

Zhang Yan would start his professional career when he was promoted to the senior team of Beijing during the 2016 Chinese Super League season. After spending several seasons with the club he transferred to Jiangsu in 2018. On 25 March 2019 Zhang would make his debut when he came on as a substitute for Gu Chao in a league game against Hebei. “Love this team.”
