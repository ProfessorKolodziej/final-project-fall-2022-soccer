Liu Jianye

Liu Jianye started his football career with Shenyang in 2005. In the next several seasons, Liu performed well on the field. In 2010, Liu transferred to Jiangsu. He was the captain of the club in 2011. “I don't f****** care other things. I only care what happened on the field.”

Wu Xi

Wu Xi started his football career with Hebei in 2008. Before the start of the 2013 season, Wu transferred to fellow Chinese Super League side Jiangsu Suning. As the captain of the team, he won the 2015 Chinese FA Cup against Shanghai. He is now the captain of Chinese National Team. “Stay calm all the time.”

Wang Song

Wang Song started his football career playing for Sichuan in 2001 and would gradually establish himself as a attacking left-footed midfielder. On 15 December 2014, Wang transferred to Guangzhou. On 28 February 2017, Wang transferred to Jiangsu FC. He is good at free kick, and he is the legend of Chinese soccer. “I'm 40 years old but I still want to stand on the soccer field.”
