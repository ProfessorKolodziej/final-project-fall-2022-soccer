Joao Miranda

Having begun his career at Coritiba, Miranda spent the 2005-06 season with Sochaux in France. At San Paulo, he was named in the Team of the Year four consecutive times from 2007 to 2010. In 2011, he joined Atletico Madrid. On 26 July 2019, he joined Jiangsu FC. Miranda is a member of Brazilian National Team. “Enjoy this moment.”
