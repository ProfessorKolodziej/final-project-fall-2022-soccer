Born in Brazil, Eder moved to Italy at age 19, where he spent most of his professional career. Ivan Santini

Croatian footballer Ivan Santini, originally from Zadar, has navigated a diverse career spanning clubs including Red Bull Salzburg, SC Freiburg, and Caen, showcasing his goal-scoring prowess and achieving milestones in various European leagues. On 29 July 2019, Santini joined Jiangsu FC. “Each moment is a chance to turn the tide.”

Huang Zichang

Huang Zichang started his football career when he signed his first professional contract with Chinese Super League side Jiangsu Suning in 2016. Huang made his senior debut and scored his first goal in a league game on 4 March 2018 in a 3:1 away win against Guizhou. “Jiangsu Fighting!”
